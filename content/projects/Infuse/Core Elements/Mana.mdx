---
title: Magic Energy
description: The basics of magic energy usage for Infuse
---

Magic energy is the base power source for all machines and tools. At first, it will come from the player directly, then will transfer into an automated source.
It's core types are : 
- None (default)
- Earth
- Wind
- Water
- Fire
- Lightning
- Void

They are in order of acquiring difficulty, due to zone progression.
If fired, it forms a gentle beam, powering any machine standing in it's path.
