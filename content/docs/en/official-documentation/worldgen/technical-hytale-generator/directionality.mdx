### Static

Allows you to specify the single direction the Prop will be placed in.

Parameters:

<table><thead><tr><th width="160">Name</th><th width="305.35638427734375">Type</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Rotation</td><td>integer (optional, defaults to 0)</td><td>Allowed values: <code>0</code>, <code>90</code>, <code>180</code>, <code>270</code>.</td></tr><tr><td>Pattern</td><td>Pattern slot</td><td>Used to locate a suitable position for the Prop. Does not affect the Prefab’s direction.</td></tr></tbody></table>

***

### Random

Gives the Prefab random directions, based on a seed.

Parameters:

<table><thead><tr><th width="160">Name</th><th width="141.871337890625">Type</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Seed</td><td>string</td><td>Affects rotations.</td></tr><tr><td>Pattern</td><td>Pattern slot</td><td>Used to locate a suitable position for the Prop. Does not affect the Prefab’s direction.</td></tr></tbody></table>

***

### Pattern _(Directionality type name)_

Allows the Prop to have the correct direction based on its environment. Allows you to link Prefab directions to Pattern assets for each direction.

Parameters:

<table><thead><tr><th width="139.089111328125">Name</th><th width="189.39605712890625">Type</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>InitialDirection</td><td>string (<code>N</code> | <code>S</code> | <code>E</code> | <code>W</code>)</td><td>The direction the Prop faces in its original state. Example: A balcony facing North means it is looking North.</td></tr><tr><td>NorthPattern</td><td>Pattern slot</td><td>Defines environments where this Prop should be placed facing North.</td></tr><tr><td>SouthPattern</td><td>Pattern slot</td><td>Defines environments where this Prop should be placed facing South.</td></tr><tr><td>EastPattern</td><td>Pattern slot</td><td>Defines environments where this Prop should be placed facing East.</td></tr><tr><td>WestPattern</td><td>Pattern slot</td><td>Defines environments where this Prop should be placed facing West.</td></tr><tr><td>Seed</td><td>string</td><td>Affects direction picking where more than one direction is possible.</td></tr></tbody></table>
